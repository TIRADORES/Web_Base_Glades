Commitment to Excellence

Glades International Corporation operates under internationally recognized quality and food safety standards to ensure product integrity and customer confidence. These certifications are regularly audited by independent third parties, reinforcing our commitment to excellence.

ISO 9001

Quality Management System

Ensures consistent product quality and continuous process improvement through a systematic approach to quality management.

  • Consistent quality standards
  • Continuous improvement processes
  • Customer satisfaction focus
  • Risk-based thinking

ISO 22000

Food Safety Management System

Guarantees safe manufacturing of food-contact packaging products through a comprehensive food safety management system.

  • Food safety hazard control
  • Prerequisite programs
  • HACCP principles
  • Interactive communication

BRC Global Standard

Food Safety / Packaging

Fully aligned with the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI), meeting global market requirements for packaging materials.

  • GFSI recognized standard
  • Hazard and risk management
  • Product safety culture
  • Global market access

AIB International

Food Contact Packaging Standards

Confirms compliance with stringent hygiene and food contact packaging standards through rigorous auditing and inspection.

  • Stringent hygiene standards
  • Food contact material safety
  • Comprehensive auditing
  • Operational excellence
