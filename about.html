About Glades International

Leading the packaging industry with innovation, quality, and sustainability for over 30 years.

Who We Are

Glades International Corporation is a premier packaging solutions provider with over three decades of excellence in the industry. Founded in 1993, we have grown from a local packaging supplier to a globally recognized leader serving clients across 50+ countries.

Our commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability has positioned us as the preferred partner for businesses seeking reliable, efficient, and environmentally conscious packaging solutions.

With over 500 skilled employees and state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, we deliver comprehensive packaging solutions that meet the evolving needs of diverse industries including food & beverage, industrial, pharmaceutical, and consumer goods.
