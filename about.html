About Glades International Corporation

Leading manufacturer of high-quality food packaging solutions since 1996

Our Story

Founded in 1996 and operational since 1997, Glades International Corporation is a leading manufacturer of high-quality food packaging solutions based in LIIP Mamplasan, Biñan, Laguna, Philippines.

From a modest beginning of 50 employees, Glades has grown into one of the country's major suppliers of thermoformed and injection-molded plastic packaging products, serving top Quick Service Restaurants (QSRs) and food packaging companies.

Today, with over 500 skilled employees, Glades continues to deliver innovative, safe, and reliable packaging solutions that meet global standards.
