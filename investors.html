Company Overview

Established in 1996 in Singapore and operational in the Philippines since 1997, Glades International Corporation specializes in food packaging solutions, thermoforming and injection-molded products for top QSR (Quick Service Restaurant) and food brands.

Today, we operate multiple facilities across China, Malaysia and the Philippines as part of the Glades Greiner Group, maintaining strong supply networks and customer relationships.

Ownership Structure

As a private company, Glades does not publicly list shareholders or trade on a stock exchange. We maintain long-term strategic partnerships with global brands and suppliers, attracting stakeholders focused on sustainable growth and market expansion.
