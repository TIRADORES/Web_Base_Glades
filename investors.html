About Glades International Corporation

Glades International Corporation is a privately-held packaging and manufacturing company with nearly three decades of operational experience in the Asia Pacific region.

Established in 1996 in Singapore and operational in the Philippines since 1997, the company specializes in food packaging solutions, thermoforming and injection-molded products for top QSR (Quick Service Restaurant) and food brands.

Today, Glades operates multiple facilities across China, Malaysia and the Philippines as part of the Glades Greiner Group, employing hundreds of people and maintaining strong supply networks and customer relationships.

Ownership Structure

As a private company, Glades does not publicly list shareholders or trade on a stock exchange. As a result, specific shareholding structures and public equity investors are not available.

However, the company maintains long-term strategic partnerships with global brands and suppliers, and attracts interest from stakeholders focused on sustainable growth, quality certification, and market expansion in packaging.
